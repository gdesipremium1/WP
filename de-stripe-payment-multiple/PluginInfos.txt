---------------------------------------
----- What this plugin manages:
-----
1 Booking for training and coaching sessions
2 Pre-payment and reservation of customer funds
3 Processing final payment upon customer satisfaction
4 Generating a final payment link, which can be sent via email or SMS
5 Sending payment emails and conducting customer satisfaction surveys
6 Automatic distribution of earnings to multiple beneficiaries (e.g., coaches and the WordPress platform)
7 Transactions:
    - Security
    - Automation
    - Seamless processing

---------------------------------------
----- About the resource code:
-----
1 WordPress WooCommerce (for payment processing)
2 Stripe payment integration using the Stripe API
3 WordPress REST API
4 Various required views and templates
